About us


Welcome to Ocean Photography by Ray Matthews

Capturing the Heart of the Ocean

At Ocean Photography by Ray Matthews, we believe that the ocean is more than just a body of water; it is a vast, living canvas, rich with unseen wonders and untold stories. Founded by Ray Matthews, a passionate ocean photographer with a deep love for the sea, our blog is a window into the mesmerizing world beneath the waves.

Our Journey

Ray Matthews' journey with ocean photography began over a decade ago. Fascinated by the sea from a young age, Ray turned his passion into a profession, dedicating his life to capturing the ocean's untamed beauty. His work is not just about taking pictures; it's about conveying a deeper appreciation for marine life and the importance of ocean conservation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring the ocean's hidden treasures to the surface for the world to see. Through Ray's lens, we explore the intricate dance of light and shadow underwater, the vibrant colors of marine life, and the profound moments of tranquility beneath the waves. We aim to inspire our readers to see the ocean through a new perspective and recognize its vital role in our world.
